How to Play

  • Goal: Remove numbers so rows/cols match targets.
  • Tap to remove a number.
  • Auto-Lock: When a row/col is solved (Green), remaining numbers automatically lock!
  • Manual Lock: Use the toggle button below to lock numbers manually.
  • Hint 💡: Tap the bulb to automatically lock one correct number for you.
  • Warning: Removing a correct number costs a Life ❤.
  • Easy Mode 🛟: If you lose a life, you can enable Easy Mode to see the sum of your locked numbers.
