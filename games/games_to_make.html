  • Secret Agent Racer: Drive a high-tech sports car down a scrolling highway, using gadgets like oil slicks and smoke screens to defeat enemy vehicles. A clone of Spy Hunter.
  • Leap and Crash: Drive a car in a top-down view, bumping opponents into obstacles and jumping over large gaps in the road to reach the finish line. A clone of Bump 'n' Jump.
  • Wasteland Car Battler: Build and upgrade a custom combat vehicle to fight in arena duels and survive on the dangerous highways of a dystopian future. A clone of Autoduel.
  • Lunar Buggy Patrol: Drive a six-wheeled rover across the moon's surface, jumping over craters and shooting at alien ships attacking from above. A clone of Moon Patrol.
  • Jungle Treasure Run: Run through a dangerous jungle, swinging on vines over crocodile pits and dodging rolling logs to collect treasure within a time limit. A clone of Pitfall.
  • Critter Saver: Guide a horde of mindless, green-haired creatures to safety by assigning them specific skills like digging, building stairs, or blocking paths. A clone of Lemmings.
