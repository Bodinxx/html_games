GEM PILLARS

Score: 0

Arrows to Move • Up to Rotate

How to Play

  • Control the falling pillar of 3 gems.
  • Match 3 or more gems of the same color to clear them.
  • Matches can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  • Clear gems to cause chain reactions!

Controls

  • ⬅️ ➡️ Move Left/Right
  • ⬇️ Drop Faster
  • ⬆️ or Space to Rotate Colors
