How to Play

Connect tiles by dragging across adjacent cells (including diagonals).

🟡 Start with any tile.
🔗 Each next tile must be the same number OR double the previous.
✨ Need at least 2 tiles to score.
💥 All connected tiles are cleared and you score their sum!

Build longer chains for bigger combos. Try to chain multiples: 4 → 4 → 8 → 16…
