How to Play

Connect tiles by dragging across adjacent cells — including diagonals.

🟡 Start on any tile.
🔗 Each next tile must be the same or double the previous.
✨ You need at least 2 tiles to score.
💥 All connected tiles are cleared — you score their sum!
🏆 Chains of 5+ earn a length multiplier bonus.
📈 Advance levels as your score grows — bigger tiles appear!
🗑️ At levels 3, 6, 12, 24, 48 & 96 the lowest tile is purged from the board!
💾 Hit Save to store your progress in this browser.
