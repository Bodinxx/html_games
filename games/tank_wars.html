GAME GUIDE

Objective: Destroy the enemy tank before they destroy you!

  • Aiming: Adjust your turret Angle and shot Power using the controls.
  • Moving: Use A/D keys or On-Screen Arrows to drive. Movement is limited by Fuel.
  • Wind: Watch the wind indicator at the top. It pushes shells left or right.
    Easy: Static Wind | Medium: Small Shifts | Hard: Chaos
  • Terrain: The ground is destructible. You can dig holes, create barriers, or bury your opponent.
  • Burying: If hit by a Dirtball, a tank may get stuck underground. You cannot move while buried! Shoot the ground above you to escape.
  • Controls: Use Screen Buttons or Keyboard (Arrow Keys + Space).
