How to Play
  • Start: Roll a 6 to move a piece out of your base.
  • Move: Pieces move clockwise. Roll a 6 for an extra turn!
  • Capture: Land on an opponent to send them back to base. ★ Stars are Safe Zones.
  • Win: Move all 4 pieces to Home. You must roll the exact number to enter Home.
