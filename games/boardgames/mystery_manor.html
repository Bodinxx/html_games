Investigator's Manual

1. The Goal

A crime has occurred! Inside a Confidential File are three cards: one Suspect, one Location, and one Tool. Your mission is to be the first to identify these three cards. If a player (including you) holds a card in their hand, that card cannot be in the secret file.

2. Movement & The Manor

A

Roll the dice to see how far you can move. You must use all your moves unless you enter a room.

B

Rooms are entered through White Lines (Doors). You stop movement immediately upon entering.

C

You must leave your current tile to end your turn. No loitering in the same spot!

3. Questioning Guests

When you enter a room, you make a Suggestion (e.g., "I suggest Baron Birch in the Library with the Dagger").

  • The player to your left checks their hand for any of those three cards.
  • If they have one, they show it only to you. Your notebook will mark this automatically.
  • If they don't, the question moves to the next player.
  • If no one has the cards, you've likely found the solution!

4. Using Your Notebook

Labels

Click a Name (e.g. 'Baron Birch') to cycle states: Crossed out (ruled out), Circled (high priority), or Normal.

Cells (P1-P8)

Click a cell to mark ✓ (player has it) or ✕ (player doesn't). Use numbers 1-6 to group possibilities.

5. Making an Accusation

When you are certain, click Make Accusation. Enter the Suspect, Location, and Tool. Warning: If you are wrong, you are eliminated from the game!
