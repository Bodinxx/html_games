How to Play

Standard Cards

  • ⇄ U-Turn: Reverse order.
  • Ø Block: Skip next player.
  • +2 Plus 2: Penalty card.

New Tech

  • 🪞 Mirror: Auto-reflect penalties.
  • ❄️ Freeze: Skip next for 2 turns.
  • 🎁 Gift: Give card to next.

Special Tech

  • 🌀 Vortex: Rotate all hands.
  • 📡 Pulse: Everyone draws 1.
  • 🧲 Magnet: Force draw until color match.
  • 🕵️ Spy: Peek at smallest hand.
  • 🎲 Chaos: Reshuffle all hands.
