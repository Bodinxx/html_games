Technical Manual

Core Directive

Outpace the Neural-X AI to reach exactly 1000 Distance Units. The circuit operates on a state-based protocol where hazards block progress and shields provide immunity.

Card Glossary

⚡ Distance (25, 50, 75, 100, 200)

Advances your unit count. Requires 'GO' status and zero active inhibitors. If hit by JAM, you cannot play cards over 50.

🛑 Inhibitors (Hazards)

STOP halts progress. DRAIN empties power. CRASH locks the OS. JAM restricts transmission (speed limit).

🟢 Protocols (Remedies)

GO clears Stop. CHARG clears Drain. BOOT clears Crash. CLEAR clears Jam.

🛡️ Neural Shields (Safeties)

Permanent passive upgrades. SOLAR (Drain immunity), WALL (Crash immunity), LINK (Jam immunity), PILOT (Stop immunity + permanent GO).

Operational Sequence

  • 1. START: You cannot move until you sync a GO protocol.
  • 2. SYNC: Play a card to affect your stats or disrupt the Opponent.
  • 3. PURGE (🗑️): Discard a card to end your turn. Useful for cycling dead data.
  • 4. RE-SYNC: Draw a new card from the Core Deck after your turn or manually if below hand limit.
