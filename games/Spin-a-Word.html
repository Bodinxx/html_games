HOW TO PLAY

The Objective

Guess the hidden phrase by spinning for money and revealing letters. Solve the puzzle to win your accumulated cash!

Turn Actions

  • SPIN: Determines the cash value for each consonant you guess correctly. Careful of "Bankrupt" or "Lose a Turn"!
  • VOWEL: Buy a vowel (A, E, I, O, U) for a flat rate of $250. You must have the funds to buy.
  • SOLVE: Think you know the phrase? Type it in exactly to win the game. Incorrect solves end your turn.

The Opponents

You are playing against Beep-Boop and Mega-Byte. They are AI bots that will take their turns automatically. They can spin, guess, and even solve the puzzle if they get smart enough!
