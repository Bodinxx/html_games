Mission Data

Power-Ups (Buffs)

Wide Paddle
Expands paddle size for better coverage.
Multi-Ball
Splits every active ball into three. Chaos ensues!
Laser Cannon
Equips dual cannons. Click or Press Space to fire.
Magnet
Balls stick to the paddle. Click to launch them.
Drill
Balls turn orange and cut through ANY brick without bouncing.
Safety Net
Creates a forcefield at the bottom for 10 seconds.

Hazards (Debuffs)

Shrink
Reduces paddle size significantly. Avoid at all costs.
Fast Ball
Increases ball speed instantly.
Chaos
Inverts Left/Right controls for 5 seconds.
Slow Motion
Makes the paddle move sluggishly.

Level Mechanics

Grey Metal Blocks: These are indestructible to normal balls. They will deflect your shots with a metal clank. Use the Drill power-up to destroy them for bonus points. You do not need to destroy these to clear the level.
